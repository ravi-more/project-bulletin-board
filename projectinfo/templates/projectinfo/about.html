About

This project is developed with a focus to simplify and streamline the the process of the "Final year Project topic application" for teachers as well as students. Here, we have worked to avoid problems like project repetition. The system shows all topics via a bulletin board. Pending and approved projects fashion distinct bulletin boards for simplification purposes.
